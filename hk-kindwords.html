Words
These lovely blurbs are all from people who've seen me day-in, day-out, doing the job well.
Henry Kember worked with me on one of the most terrifyingly complex projects I’d ever led at Droga. And that’s how I know how good he is. He had a reputation as a writer’s writer and whip smart. When we worked together, I found both those things to be true – along with many other strengths. I was his Group Creative director at the time, and together we were tackling that fateful project, which went on to become very well awarded, earning recognition in categories Droga work rarely gets to enter. In that time and in that process, I not only got the chance to experience Henry’s writing skills firsthand. I also came to see how good he is at managing an unwieldy project with lots of arms. Given how effectively Henry managed that crazy experience and how much he contributed, I can happily recommend him as a powerful addition to any creative department.
- Juliana Cobb, ECD 72andsunny New York
