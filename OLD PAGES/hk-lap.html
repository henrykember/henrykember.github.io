Lap
Electro-
acoustic
Installation
A thing I did way back when, exploring the layers of repetitive rhythm in the act of lane swimming. Moving the first rope triggers a sound. Move the second, trigger video. Move the third, modulate the effects on both. NB this was done way before I had any idea how to document things, and a full three years before I got into advertising and the magical world of case studies.
Henry Kember 2016
