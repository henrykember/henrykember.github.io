Telstra Air
Public Light Installation
Telstra turned all their old phone boxes into a network of public wifi spots. But when did you last notice a phone box? To launch Telstra Air, we made the invisible visible. Collaborating with Glue Society to create a spectacular installation across the Melbourne and Sydney CBDs: light streams representing Wi-Fi streams, coordinated over a purpose-built Wi-Fi network.
Henry Kember 2018
